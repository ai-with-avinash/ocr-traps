Thus, using the Limit Laws, we get

$$
\frac {d}{d x} \left(\frac {u}{v}\right) = \frac {v \lim _ {\Delta x \to 0} \frac {\Delta u}{\Delta x} - u \lim _ {\Delta x \to 0} \frac {\Delta v}{\Delta x}}{v \lim _ {\Delta x \to 0} (v + \Delta v)} = \frac {v \frac {d u}{d x} - u \frac {d v}{d x}}{v ^ {2}}
